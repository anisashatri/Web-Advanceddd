Lionel Messi's Final Game

On December 18, 2022, the world witnessed history as Lionel Messi led Argentina to victory in one of the most dramatic World Cup finals ever. Facing France in Lusail Stadium, Messi scored twice and guided his team through a thrilling 3–3 draw that ended with Argentina winning 4–2 on penalties.

This moment marked the completion of his legendary career — the ultimate prize finally achieved. The victory sealed his legacy as one of the greatest footballers of all time.
